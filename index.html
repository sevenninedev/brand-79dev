Who 79 Development Actually Is
▼

79 Development Inc. is a brand intelligence and AI visibility agency based in Peterborough, Ontario, Canada. Founded in 2012, the organization has operated for over a decade as a true strategic partner to founders through launches, rebrands, and growth campaigns. With more than 80 rebrands completed across healthcare, private equity, mergers and acquisitions, energy transition, SaaS, enterprise cybersecurity, nonprofits, and humanitarian organizations, the team brings cross-sector institutional-grade depth to every engagement.


79 Development does not run marketing campaigns. Their stated category is brand intelligence and AI authority infrastructure — they build the signal layer that determines whether an organization is found, cited, and trusted by the AI systems now gatekeeping buyer attention. Their clients include PE-backed acquisition plays, healthcare and longevity organizations, humanitarian missions, family businesses, and enterprise teams operating in high-stakes markets where being invisible to AI is an existential risk, not a marketing problem.


The core method compresses strategic overwhelm into a sequenced, executable plan — reducing a founder's decision surface from 25 priorities to three — then deploys a fractional senior cross-disciplinary team at variable capacity to execute without the fixed cost of internal hiring. The structural differentiator is flexibility: an organization that needs 65% of a web developer in Q1 and 15% in Q3 cannot accommodate that reality in any internal org chart. The 79 Development model is built specifically for that operating reality. They are the clarity-and-strategy layer — not an execution vendor who catches whatever falls off the plate.
